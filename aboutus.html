About Us

Welcome Accurate Control

Innovative Automation Solutions for Modern Industries

M/s Accurate Control & Automation was established in November 2012 with a clear vision to advance automation across Indian industries. Founded by Mr. Suresh Chand and Mr. Manoranjan Prasad, who together bring over 27 years of extensive technical and marketing experience in the automation sector, the company is built on expertise, commitment, and reliability. We specialize in providing the most suitable automation components tailored to specific industrial applications, supported by accurate and prompt service. Beyond fulfilling customer requirements, we proactively guide clients toward efficient, reliable, and future-ready solutions that ensure long-term performance and growth. Today, Accurate Control & Automation is a well-known and respected name in the automation fraternity. Our strength lies in our highly skilled technical team of engineers who ensure timely project completion and smooth operational performance. Our vision is to achieve sustainable and collective growth alongside our valued customers and trusted vendors, building long-term partnerships based on trust, quality, and commitment.

MISSION
To provide high-quality automation components and engineered solutions tailored to industry needs, backed by technical expertise, prompt service, and long-term customer support that ensures efficiency, reliability, and operational excellence.
VISION
To become a leading and trusted automation solutions provider in India by delivering innovative, reliable, and future-ready industrial technologies that drive sustainable growth for our customers and partners.
VALUES
Quality Excellence
We commit to delivering products that meet the highest standards of safety, performance, and reliability.
