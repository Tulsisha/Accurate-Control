SIEMENS PLC AUTOMATION SOLUTIONS

We provide advanced Siemens PLC based automation solutions including S7-200 Smart, S7-1200, S7-1500 and ET200SP series. These controllers are widely used in industrial process control, machine automation, utilities, and infrastructure projects.

Our PLC systems ensure reliable operation, real-time monitoring, precise control logic execution, and seamless integration with HMI, SCADA and remote monitoring platforms.

Key Features

  • S7-200 Smart – Compact PLC for small automation systems
  • S7-1200 – Modular controller with PROFINET communication
  • S7-1500 – High-performance PLC for complex processes
  • ET200SP – Distributed I/O system for scalable automation
  • Integrated with HMI, SCADA & SQL Data Logging
